date: 2026-06-28

Seven days until the proposal defense. Surprisingly I didn't fall into the usual all-nighter spiral this week — instead I forced myself into bed before eleven, because I noticed derivations done at 2am almost always need to be redone the next morning anyway.

My advisor mentioned, almost offhand, in the office today: "you could sidestep that identification condition entirely with a different prior assumption." Ten minutes, and a problem I'd been stuck on for three days was gone. It struck me that a big part of growing through a PhD isn't learning to solve problems alone — it's learning to ask, in time.

Finally got the framework diagram for the belief-updating chapter to look right tonight, ate convenience-store oden while I did it. The hallway was quiet, and outside the wind had that thick, pre-storm heaviness to it.
